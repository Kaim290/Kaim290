Hello!

Hey, I’m Kai. I’m a 22-year-old game developer and environment artist with a background in Computer Science. I love creating immersive worlds, building systems from scratch, and bringing ideas to life inside Unreal Engine. Ever since I was little, my brother and I have been making video games together. We started in Unity and eventually moved to Unreal Engine. I'm an artist at heart, and I love taking the ideas in my head and turning them into something real—that's what keeps me passionate about game development.

My projects range from replicated multiplayer dungeon crawlers to narrative-driven solo horror experiences. I create all of my assets and environments from scratch, giving each project its own visual identity.

I'm always looking to grow, learn more, refine my skills, and create better, higher-quality projects.

Phyics Handle

I implemented a physics-based interaction system allowing the player to pick up, move, and throw various objects—crates, furniture, and environmental props. One early limitation was that players could stand on objects they were holding, enabling unintended movement exploits.

Spawning System

Multiplayer Gameplay

Once all players join a session, they can freely explore the world together. Player animations, item interactions, and enemy AI all replicate seamlessly across the server, creating a smooth and synchronized multiplayer experience.

Maps


Forest
The forest uses an expanded version of the tile-based generation system from Unhallowed. Instead of placing individual tiles, the algorithm assembles full level chunks, allowing for larger, more natural outdoor environments with greater variation each run..


Town
The town is a fully handcrafted environment featuring a fort, central settlement, train-station-style hub, and entry point to an underground mine system. All major structures and props were built and arranged manually.


Mines
The mines extend from the edge of town into a dark, branching tunnel network. Players search for dynamite to clear blocked passages while an unseen threat stalks them from the shadows, heightening the tension as they progress.


Skin Customization


A fully replicated customization system allows players to personalize their character’s appearance with different skins and cosmetic variations, all synchronized across the network for other players to see.


Chaos Physics!
This demo showcases Unreal Engine’s Chaos physics system. Objects fracture into smaller simulated pieces, creating dynamic destruction effects that can be used for clearing blocked pathways or adding environmental interaction.

Barriers
A barrier system. You can place materials, and once they're placed enemeies can destroy them. Once they're destroyed you can rebuild them without having to reexpend the materials.
